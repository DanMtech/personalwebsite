Tabletop Roleplaying Games
For over 10 years, I have been a Game Master (GM) for several different Tabletop Roleplaying Games (TTRPGs). I run sessions weekly with my friends as players, and it is one of my favorite hobbies. I started using Dungeons and Dragons 5th Edition, but have since expanded and have played using several systems.
